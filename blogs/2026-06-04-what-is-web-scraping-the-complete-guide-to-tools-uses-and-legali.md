Web scraping is the automated process of extracting data from websites and converting it into structured formats like spreadsheets or databases, and this guide covers how it works, the types of tools available from no-code platforms to Python libraries, and common applications like price monitoring and lead generation. The guide notes that 65% of AI teams already use automated data collection and that scraping publicly accessible data is generally legal. Compliance with Terms of Service and data privacy laws like GDPR remains essential for responsible data collection.
