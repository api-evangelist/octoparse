Octoparse offers a perpetually free tier plus paid plans starting at $83/month for Standard ($69 billed annually) and $299/month for Professional ($249 annually). The free plan includes 10 scraping tasks, local-only extraction, and up to 50,000 monthly data rows, while cloud extraction, scheduling, and advanced anti-blocking require a paid plan. All paid subscriptions include a five-day refund guarantee.
